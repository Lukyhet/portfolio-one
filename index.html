About Us

Early Bird Yoga was founded by a small group of Yoga enthusiast who understand the value of time and decided to create a space for yogis who prefer to have an early practice due to their busy jobs and family lives but don´t want to quit their daily Yoga practice

At Early Bird Yoga we believe in the power of starting the day with a healthy yoga routine, with that in mind we have curated specific Yoga programs for our dear early birds. Join us!

What We Offer

To start your Yoga journey with us select your Yoga program according to your level and preference. Get in shape with the relaxing flow of yoga, gain elasticity and confidence while preparing to start your day from the comfort of your home.
